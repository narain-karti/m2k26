INTRODUCTION

About The Symposium

The Department of Naval Architecture and Offshore Engineering of AMET Deemed to be University is organizing a National Level Technical Symposium MEREDITH 2K26 on 06 March, 2026.


The symposium brings together experts, researchers, practitioners, and professionals to deliberate upon the various aspects of quality in higher education. It serves as a platform to identify areas of potential upgradation, facilitate exchange of stakeholder inputs, and create qualitative value from the synergy of pluralistic ideas.

UNIVERSITY

About AMET

AMET is India's first Deemed to be University in Maritime Education, ranked 3rd globally in PIMET rankings by IAMU. Established in 1993, AMET has consistently fed the global shipping industry with unrivalled human resources. It holds recognitions like NIRF, ARIIA, DGS-CIP, and is accredited by NAAC with 'A' Grade.

VISION

To sustain identity as a World Class Leader in Maritime Education and empower learners with wholesome knowledge through progressive innovation in training, research, and development.

MISSION

Impart value-based higher education and technical knowledge. Emerge as a Centre of Excellence in recent technologies. Create world-class research capabilities.

ACADEMICS

Department of Naval Architecture

Established in 2007, the Department offers Ph.D., M.E., and B.E. programs. Accredited by the NBA from 2024, it boasts a strong placement record and renowned faculty. Research focus includes offshore structures, naval architecture, and marine engineering.

DEPT VISION

To sustain identity as a World Class Leader in Maritime Education and empower learners with wholesome knowledge.

DEPT MISSION

To impart quality education producing innovative, socio-economically conscious Naval Architects. To provide knowledge essential for local and global demands.
